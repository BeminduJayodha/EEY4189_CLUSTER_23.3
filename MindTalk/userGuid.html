Patient Guide

Instructions:

  1. Visit our website
  2. Click on the "Sign Up" button.
  3. Choose "Patient" as your user type.
  4. Fill out the registration form with your username, password, and email.
  5. Click the "SIGN UP" button to create your account.
  6. Once registered, log in to your account using your credentials.
  7. Browse the list of available counselors.
  8. Select a counselor and choose a suitable time for your counseling session.
  9. On the scheduled date and time, log in to your account to access the chat for counseling.

Counselor Guide & Registration

Thank you for considering joining our counseling platform as a counselor. Follow the instructions below to get started:

Instructions:

  1. Visit our website
  2. Click on the "Sign Up" button.
  3. Choose "Counselor" as your user type.
  4. Fill out the registration form with your username, password, and email.
  5. Click the "SIGN UP" button to create your account.
  6. Once registered, log in to your account using your credentials.
  7. Then you have to prove your qualifications, for that fill out the registration form

Scheduling Appointments:

  1. After successful registration, click on the "Schedule Appointment" link.
  2. Choose the date and time slots when you are available for counseling.
  3. Patients will view your availability and book appointments accordingly.

Thank you for your commitment to providing quality counseling services on our platform. If you have any questions or issues, feel free to contact our support team.
